Our Mission

At Players Club Management, we are dedicated to empowering student-athletes by maximizing their Name, Image, and Likeness (NIL) opportunities. Our mission is to help athletes build their personal brands, secure meaningful partnerships, and create long-term financial success through strategic marketing and brand collaborations. We believe that every athlete deserves the opportunity to capitalize on their hard work and dedication, and we are here to guide them every step of the way.

Our Story

Players Club Management was founded with a passion for sports and a vision to help athletes navigate the evolving NIL landscape. As former athletes and business professionals, we recognized the need for a trusted, athlete-first agency that provides guidance, support, and expertise in the NIL space. Our journey began with a commitment to ensuring that student-athletes are not only compensated fairly but also educated on financial literacy, brand building, and long-term career planning. Today, we are proud to help athletes turn their talents into sustainable success.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

▶ What services does Players Club Management offer?

We provide NIL deal negotiation, brand partnership facilitation, marketing strategy development, financial literacy education, and personal brand coaching for student-athletes.

▶ Who can sign with Players Club Management?

We work with high school and college athletes across various sports who are looking to maximize their NIL potential.

▶ How does Players Club Management get paid?

We offer a tiered membership system, including a free entry-level tier. For higher-tier memberships, we require a $100 consulting fee deposit and take a 20% commission on any NIL deals secured.

▶ How can brands collaborate with Players Club Management?

Brands looking to partner with our athletes can reach out through our "Connect With Us" button or email us directly. We will match brands with the right athletes based on target audience, values, and campaign goals.

▶ Do you provide legal support for NIL contracts?

Yes, we work with legal professionals to ensure all NIL contracts are reviewed and compliant with NCAA and state regulations.
